14th April 2018: La Vie d'Adèle – Chapitres 1 & 2 / Blue is the Warmest Colour (2013)

Masterfully executed, many shots and scenes brought back good memories in ways I could not quite put into words. Blue is the Warmest Colour (directed, co-written and co-produced by Abdellatif Kechiche) tells the coming-of-age story of Adèle, a high school girl from a middle-class family, who met and fell in love with Emma, a graduating art student, and their life together, and the inevitable divergence later in life.

Some of the intimate scenes would sure linger in your mind for days to come.
