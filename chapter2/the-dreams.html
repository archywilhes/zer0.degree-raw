Still Life with Grape Juice and Sandwiches (1994) by David Ligare
15 Jun 2018 10min read

The Dreams 《梦》

Oh. All these recurring dreams. How I wish I never wake up from them. And how I wish the recurring comes to an end. In every dream you look lovelier than ever. In every rousing I miss you more. It pains me to go into slumber every night only to find you by my side for such a brief moment. And then you are gone, as the constructed world unfolds itself into pieces of broken memories.

But the you in my dreams are not really you. It is an idealised version of you. The you who look forwards to a future where we can live happily. The you who always smile. The you who are always optimistic and don’t give up easily. There were times when you were this person, and you loved it. But being this person ain’t no easy task, especially given the circumstances we were in. How I wish I were more caring and less demanding and made more rooms for your personal space and gave you a hand when you were outside of your comfort zone so you could try your best to be this person. How I wish I knew when you were feeling insecure and gave you the courage and warmth you needed, like you did for me when I was feeling insecure. How I wish I were a better lover.

I have not yet found a way to contact you after you disappeared from my world half a year ago, which is perhaps the best thing for the two of us who have been deeply hurt by one another. I wish that the real you are doing well in life.

• • •

这些重复的梦。我多么希望无须从中醒觉。我也多么希望能过彻底摆脱。梦境中的你每次都比之前动人。某名醒来的我每天都比昨天更想念你。這是一种折磨。每夜与你梦中的相遇是如此的短暂。然后你就消失了，随着这个构造的现实倒塌，留下满地记忆的碎片。

當然梦中的你並不是真实的你。她是我理想中的你。那個对我们未來的生活充滿著希望的你。那個经常帶着笑容的你。那个乐观而且从不轻易放弃的你。這個你是我最喜歡的你，也是你最喜歡的自己。可是要把这个自己带出来并不容易（尤其考虑到我们一起生活时的场景与事态）。我多么希望过去的我会更懂得照顾你。会更懂得给你空间呼吸。当你试图走出『心理舒适区』时，懂得给你勇气与鼓励。懂得知道什么时候你缺乏安全感。懂得给你温暖，给你自信,就如同当你意识到我安全感较低的时候。How I wish I were a better lover.

半年已经过去了。自从你从我的世界消失后，我还没找到方式联系你。这对我们两人是一件好事吧，毕竟我们互相伤害所带来的疤痕仍然清晰可见。I wish that the real you are doing well in life.
